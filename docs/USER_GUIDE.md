## Getting Started

### Home Page
When you open the application, you'll see four main tools:
1. **Activity Builder** - Create matching, sorting, and choice activities
2. **AAC Board Builder** - Design communication boards with symbols and audio
3. **Visual Schedule Creator** - Build step-by-step daily routines
4. **My Library** - View and manage all your created content

## Creating an AAC Board

### Step 1: Open AAC Board Builder
Click on "AAC Board Builder" from the home page.

### Step 2: Configure Your Board
- **Board Name**: Give your board a meaningful name (e.g., "Meal Time Communication")
- **Grid Size**: Choose from 2√ó2, 3√ó3, or 4√ó4 layouts

### Step 3: Add Symbols
1. Click on any empty cell
2. Browse symbols by category (Food, Transport, Festival, etc.)
3. Use the search bar to find specific symbols
4. Click on a symbol to add it to the cell

### Step 4: Customize Labels
- Each cell shows the symbol with a text label
- You can edit the label by clicking on it
- The label is used for text-to-speech audio

### Step 5: Test Audio
Click the speaker icon on any cell to hear the text spoken aloud using your device's text-to-speech.

### Step 6: Save and Share
- Click **Save** to store your board locally
- Click **Share** to get a link you can send via WhatsApp
- Click **Export PDF** to download a printable version

## Creating a Visual Schedule

### Step 1: Open Schedule Builder
Click on "Visual Schedule Creator" from the home page.

### Step 2: Name Your Schedule
Enter a name like "Morning Routine" or "School Day Schedule"

### Step 3: Add Steps
1. Click **Add Step**
2. Select a symbol from the library
3. The step is added to your schedule
4. Repeat to add more steps

### Step 4: Reorder Steps
Use the grip handles to drag steps up or down to change their order.

### Step 5: Preview
The right panel shows how the schedule will look to parents and children.

### Step 6: Save and Share
- **Save**: Store the schedule locally
- **Share**: Get a mobile-friendly link for parents
- **Export PDF**: Download a printable version

## Creating Activities

### Step 1: Open Activity Builder
Click on "Activity Builder" from the home page.

### Step 2: Choose Activity Type
Select from:
- **Matching Activity**: Match similar items
- **Sorting Activity**: Group items by category
- **Choice Selection**: Choose between options

### Step 3: Add Symbols
1. Click **Add Symbol**
2. Browse or search for symbols
3. Add multiple symbols to create your activity

### Step 4: Save and Share
Use the same Save, Share, and Export options as other tools.

## Tips for Best Results

### For AAC Boards
- Start with 2√ó2 for beginners, expand to 3√ó3 or 4√ó4 as needed
- Group related symbols together (e.g., all food items)
- Use clear, simple labels
- Test the audio before sharing

### For Visual Schedules
- Keep schedules short (5-8 steps) for younger children
- Use consistent symbols for recurring activities
- Order steps chronologically
- Include transition activities (e.g., "Get ready to go")

### For Activities
- Use culturally familiar items
- Mix familiar and new concepts
- Keep activities focused on one skill
- Use high-contrast, clear images

## Offline Usage

After your first visit:
- All symbols are cached locally
- You can create and edit without internet
- Saved items are stored in your browser
- Share links require internet to access
